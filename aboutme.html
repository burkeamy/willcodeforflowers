About Me and This Site

This blog is set up to be a weekly posting. I will alternate between something I am passionate and knowledgeable about, plants and landscaping, with something new I am learning, coding and web development. Check back every week to see my new post!

I do not claim to be an expert on plants and landscaping. I am just trying to share my knowledge and experience from working in the industry for many years. Plants are awesome and if I can help someone grow more flowers I'll be happy.

I wanted to become a web developer several years ago. I started by taking some classes at my local community college. Then I became a flight attendant and the learning stopped. Now I am trying to get back into it. I attended a boot camp. Currently, I am two years into my coding revival and still looking for my first professional developer job. The purpose of the coding sections is to remind my future self some things I have learned along the way.
